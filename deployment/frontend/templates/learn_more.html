Los Angeles Ground-Level Ozone Forecast | About

  • More than 20 years of historical air quality data were collected from the South Coast Air Quality Management District's monitoring site in Downtown Los Angeles.
  • A subset (>117k hourly data points) of the data from 2000 - 2015 was used to train a long short-term memory (LSTM) neural network in predicting surface-level ozone.
  • The model was validated using a smaller subset (>39k hourly data points) of the data from 2015 - 2020.
  • Predictions with a 1-hour horizon had a mean error of ±4.1 ppb, while those on a 24-hour horizon had a mean error of ±8.6 ppb.
  • New data is collected hourly from the SCAQMD website. A new forecast is issued hourly.
